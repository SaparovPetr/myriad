# Shrink - уменьшать

1. shrink in size
2. shrink rapidly
3. shrink dramatically
4. shrink to nothing
5. shrink from view
6. shrink the gap
7. shrink the budget

- Present Perfect: The company has shrunk its workforce dramatically over the last year.
- Past Simple: My wool sweater shrank in the wash.
- Past Continuous: The ice cube was shrinking rapidly under the hot sun.
- Past Perfect: By the time we arrived, the puddle had shrunk to almost nothing.
