# cater - обслуживать

1. cater to needs
2. cater for events
3. cater a party
4. cater to tastes
5. cater a wedding
6. cater to demands
7. cater a dinner

- Present Perfect: They have catered to the needs of elderly clients for years.
- Past Simple: The company catered a large corporate event last weekend.
- Past Continuous: While we were preparing the venue, the team was catering for three hundred guests.
- Past Perfect: By the time the guests arrived, the staff had already catered the entire dinner.
