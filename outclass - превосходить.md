# outclass - превосходить

1. Outclass the competition
2. Outclass the opponent
3. Outclass the field
4. Outclass everyone
5. Outclass rivals
6. Outclass the rest
7. Outclass the other teams

- Present Perfect: Their new model has completely outclassed all its rivals in the market.
- Past Simple: The young chess prodigy outclassed the experienced grandmaster in just twenty moves.
- Past Continuous: While our old software was struggling, their new program was outclassing it in every test.
- Past Perfect: By the time the race ended, the new champion had outclassed every other driver on the track.
