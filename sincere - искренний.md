# Sincere - искренний

1. sincere apology
2. sincere thanks
3. sincere condolences
4. sincere hope
5. sincere belief
6. sincere regret
7. sincere wish

- I have offered my sincere apology to the manager for the mistake. (Present Perfect)
- She expressed her sincere thanks to everyone who helped with the event. (Past Simple)
- During the funeral, he was offering his sincere condolences to the widow. (Past Continuous)
- They had already expressed their sincere hope for a peaceful resolution before the meeting ended. (Past Perfect)
