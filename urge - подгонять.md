# urge - подгонять

1. urge someone to do something
2. urge caution
3. urge action
4. strongly urge
5. feel an urge
6. sudden urge
7. resist the urge

- Present Perfect: She has urged him to reconsider his decision many times.
- Past Simple: The doctor urged caution when returning to exercise.
- Past Continuous: He was urging action on the climate bill throughout his speech.
- Past Perfect: I had felt a sudden urge to call her, but then I remembered we were arguing.
