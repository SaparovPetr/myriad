# Plainly - очевидно

You are plainly wrong.

I thought it will be a plainly decision. But I was wrong.

1. plainly obvious
2. plainly visible
3. plainly stated
4. plainly wrong
5. plainly understand
6. plainly see
7. plainly evident

- Present Perfect: He has plainly stated his opinion on the matter several times.
- Past Simple: The instructions were plainly wrong, so we got lost.
- Past Continuous: The sign was becoming plainly visible as the fog cleared.
- Past Perfect: She had plainly understood the rules before she decided to break them.
