# have what it takes - иметь необходимые качества

1. have what it takes to succeed
2. have what it takes to be a leader
3. have what it takes to win
4. have what it takes to make it
5. have what it takes to survive
6. have what it takes to become a champion
7. have what it takes to do the job

- Present Perfect: She has finally proven that she has what it takes to be a great manager.
- Past Simple: He showed everyone he had what it takes to win the tournament.
- Past Continuous: They were wondering if the new recruit had what it takes during the difficult training.
- Past Perfect: After he solved the crisis, we knew he had always had what it takes to lead.
