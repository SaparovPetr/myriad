# Shrill - пронзительный

- shrill sound

1. shrill cry
2. shrill voice
3. shrill whistle
4. shrill laughter
5. shrill scream
6. shrill shout
7. shrill warning

Examples:

- Present Perfect: The children have heard a shrill cry from the next door.
- Past Simple: The police had to respond to a shrill shout from the victim.
