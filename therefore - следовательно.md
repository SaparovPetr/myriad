# therefore - следовательно

1. And therefore
2. And therefore it is
3. And therefore we must
4. And therefore I conclude
5. And therefore it follows
6. And therefore the result
7. And therefore this means

- The evidence is conclusive, and therefore the court has reached a verdict.
- The road was closed, and therefore we took a detour.
- The weather was worsening, and therefore we were reconsidering our plans.
- He had forgotten his passport, and therefore he had missed his flight.
