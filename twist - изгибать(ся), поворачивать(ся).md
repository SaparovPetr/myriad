# twist - изгибать(ся), поворачивать(ся)

1. twist an ankle
2. twist the truth
3. twist and turn
4. twist someone's arm
5. twist off a cap
6. road twists and turns
7. a twist of fate

- She has twisted her ankle while hiking, so she can't walk properly.
- He twisted the truth to avoid getting into trouble.
- The path was twisting and turning through the dark forest.
- They had already twisted the cap off the bottle before I could find a glass.
