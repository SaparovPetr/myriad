# simplistic - упрощенный

1. overly simplistic
2. too simplistic
3. rather simplistic
4. somewhat simplistic
5. extremely simplistic
6. dangerously simplistic
7. a simplistic view

- Present Perfect: The solution has been overly simplistic and ignored several key factors.
- Past Simple: The report gave a rather simplistic explanation for the economic crisis.
- Past Continuous: The presenter was offering a dangerously simplistic answer while the audience was asking complex questions.
- Past Perfect: By the time the experts arrived, the manager had already proposed a somewhat simplistic plan.
