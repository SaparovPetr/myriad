# be all ears - внимательно слушать

#### Phrase Explanation: "Be All Ears"

**Meaning:**  
The phrase **"be all ears"** means to listen very attentively and carefully, showing great interest in what someone is saying.

**Example Usage:**  
- "Tell me the story—I’m all ears!"  
- "When she started talking about her trip, I was all ears."

**In Russian:**  
The equivalent phrase is **"внимательно слушать"**, which literally means "to listen carefully."
