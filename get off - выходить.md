# get off - выходить

1. Get off the bus.
2. Get off the train.
3. Get off at the next stop.
4. Get off work early.
5. Get off the phone.
6. Get off the couch.
7. Get off the subject.

- Present Perfect: She has just gotten off the phone with her boss.
- Past Simple: He got off the bus at the wrong stop yesterday.
- Past Continuous: I was getting off the train when I saw an old friend.
- Past Perfect: By the time I arrived, he had already gotten off work.
