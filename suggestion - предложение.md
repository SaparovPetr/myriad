#### Verb Tenses Examples  
- Present Perfect: She has made several helpful suggestions during the meeting.  
- Past Simple: He accepted the suggestion to change the schedule.  
- Past Continuous: They were discussing the suggestions from the team.  
- Past Perfect: By the time the project started, they had already rejected some suggestions.
