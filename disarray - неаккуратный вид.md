# disarray - неаккуратный вид

1. throw into disarray
2. in complete disarray
3. state of disarray
4. fall into disarray
5. create disarray
6. put into disarray
7. general disarray

- The librarian threw the bookshelves into disarray when she hurriedly reshelved the returns.
- At that moment, the wind was throwing the scattered papers into disarray across the yard.
- The previous intern had already thrown the record room into disarray before anyone noticed the mistake.
