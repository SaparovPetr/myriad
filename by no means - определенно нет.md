# by no means - определенно нет

1. by no means certain
2. by no means easy
3. by no means perfect
4. by no means finished
5. by no means the only
6. by no means clear
7. by no means simple

- The team has by no means finished the project yet.
- He said it was by no means easy to make that decision.
- She was explaining that the instructions were by no means clear.
- I had realized the task was by no means simple when I saw the full requirements.
