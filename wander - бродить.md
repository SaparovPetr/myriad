# wander - бродить

1. wander aimlessly
2. wander off
3. wander around
4. wander through
5. wander the streets
6. let your mind wander
7. wander away

- Present Perfect: She has wandered through that old forest many times.
- Past Simple: He wandered off from the group and got lost.
- Past Continuous: They were wandering around the city center all afternoon.
- Past Perfect: By the time we noticed, the child had already wandered away.
