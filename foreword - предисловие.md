# foreword - предисловие

1. write a foreword
2. foreword to the book
3. author's foreword
4. brief foreword
5. foreword by
6. foreword and introduction
7. foreword in the first edition

- Present Perfect: The famous critic has written a foreword for the new edition of the novel.
- Past Simple: She wrote a brief foreword to explain the context of the diary entries.
- Past Continuous: The editor was reading the author's foreword when he noticed a factual error.
- Past Perfect: By the time the book was published, the historian had already completed the foreword.
