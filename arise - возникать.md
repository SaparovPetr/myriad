# arise - возникать

1. arise from
2. arise out of
3. arise when
4. arise during
5. arise because of
6. arise naturally
7. arise unexpectedly

- A serious problem has arisen from the new software update.
- A disagreement arose out of a misunderstanding about the schedule.
- Questions were arising during the meeting when the manager suddenly left.
- The opportunity had already arisen before we were ready to take it.
