- impair the position
- Impair the situation

1. Impair vision
2. Impair judgment
3. Impair ability
4. Impair function
5. Impair performance
6. Impair health

- The fog has severely impaired visibility for drivers on the motorway.
- The head injury impaired his memory for several weeks.
- The loud noise was impairing my concentration while I tried to work.
- By the time he sought treatment, the disease had permanently impaired his motor skills.
