# up to - вплоть до

1. up to the present day
2. up to that point
3. up to a certain level
4. up to the end
5. up to one hundred people
6. up to the last minute
7. up to the manager

- Present Perfect: He has kept the details of the project secret up to the present day.
- Past Simple: She worked on the manuscript up to the end of last year.
- Past Continuous: They were adding new features up to the last minute before the launch.
- Past Perfect: By the time he arrived, the temperature had risen up to a dangerous level.
