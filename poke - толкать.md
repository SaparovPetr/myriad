# poke - толкать

1. Poke fun at
2. Poke a hole
3. Poke the fire
4. Poke around
5. Poke your head in
6. Poke someone in the ribs
7. Poke your nose into something

- They have often poked fun at his unusual hat.
- She poked a hole in the paper with her pencil.
- While he was poking the fire, a spark flew out.
- He had already poked his head in the room before he heard the shout.
