# ought to - должен быть

#### Common Phrases with "ought to"  
1. ought to know  
2. ought to do  
3. ought to be  
4. ought to have  
5. ought to try  
6. ought to help  
7. ought to consider  

#### Verb Tenses Examples  
- Present Perfect: You ought to have finished the task by now.  
- Past Simple: He ought to be more careful yesterday. (Note: "ought to" is mostly used with present/future meaning)  
- Present/Future: She ought to be at the meeting on time.  
- Past Perfect: They ought to have informed us earlier.  

**Note:** "Ought to" expresses duty, advice, or expectation, often similar to "should."
