#### Definition of "Overtake"

1. Overtake a car
2. Overtake the leader
3. Overtake a competitor
4. Overtake on the left
5. Overtake on a bend
6. Overtake quickly
7. Overtake safely

- Present Perfect: The new electric model has overtaken all its rivals in sales this quarter.
- Past Simple: A sudden feeling of dread overtook him when he entered the empty house.
- Past Continuous: She was overtaking the truck when the rain started pouring down.
- Past Perfect: By the time they reached the final lap, the champion had already overtaken every other car.
