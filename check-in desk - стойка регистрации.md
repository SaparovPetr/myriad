# check-in desk - стойка регистрации

1. approach the check-in desk
2. go to the check-in desk
3. at the check-in desk
4. check-in desk agent
5. check-in desk staff
6. reach the check-in desk
7. queue at the check-in desk

- Present Perfect: We have already been to the check-in desk and received our boarding passes.
- Past Simple: I went to the check-in desk and dropped off my luggage.
- Past Continuous: The passengers were lining up at the check-in desk when the announcement was made.
- Past Perfect: By the time I got to the airport, my friend had already waited at the check-in desk for twenty minutes.
