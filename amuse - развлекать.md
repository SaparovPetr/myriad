# amuse - развлекать

1. amuse the children
2. amuse the crowd
3. amuse the guests
4. amuse the audience
5. amuse oneself
6. amuse friends
7. amuse people

- Present Perfect: He has amused the children with his magic tricks all afternoon.
- Past Simple: The clown amused the crowd at the festival yesterday.
- Past Continuous: She was amusing the guests with funny stories when I arrived.
- Past Perfect: By the time the main act started, the warm-up comedian had already amused the audience.
