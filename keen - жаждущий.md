# keen - жаждущий

1. keen interest
2. keen eye
3. keen sense
4. keen to learn
5. keen on something
6. keen awareness
7. keen observer

- She has developed a keen interest in astronomy since she got a telescope.
- He had a keen eye for detail and spotted the error immediately.
- While they were walking, she was demonstrating a keen sense of direction.
- They had always shown a keen awareness of the potential problems before starting the project.
