# walk out - уходить

Seven simple and popular collocations with **"walk out"** in the meaning **"уходить"**:

1. walk out suddenly  
2. walk out angrily  
3. walk out of a meeting  
4. walk out on someone  
5. walk out of the room  
6. walk out during the speech  
7. walk out in protest  

Examples in different tenses:

- Present Perfect: She has walked out of the meeting twice this week.  
- Past Simple: He walked out angrily after the argument.  
- Past Continuous: They were walking out while the discussion was still ongoing.  
- Past Perfect: By the time the manager arrived, several employees had already walked out.
