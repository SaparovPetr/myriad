# obsessive - помешанный

1. obsessive thoughts
2. obsessive behavior
3. obsessive interest
4. obsessive need
5. obsessive focus
6. obsessive attention to detail
7. obsessive love

- He has developed an obsessive interest in vintage cars since he visited the museum.
- She had an obsessive need for order that everyone noticed.
- He was displaying obsessive behavior, checking the locks repeatedly.
- She realized she had had an obsessive focus on her work, neglecting everything else.
