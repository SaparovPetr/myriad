# apart from this - помимо этого

1. apart from this fact
2. apart from this issue
3. apart from this one time
4. apart from this incident
5. apart from this detail
6. apart from this problem
7. apart from this matter

- Present Perfect: She has visited many countries, but apart from this one trip to Italy, she has never been to Europe.
- Past Simple: The meeting was long and difficult, but apart from this issue, we agreed on everything.
- Past Continuous: He was explaining the complex theory, but apart from this detail, I was following his lecture.
- Past Perfect: They had finished all the preparations for the event, apart from this final matter, which was resolved later.
