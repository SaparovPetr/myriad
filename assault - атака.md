2. lead an assault

5. survive an assault
6. assault the enemy

- Past Simple: The general led a brutal assault against the enemy camp last night.
- Past Continuous: The troops were mounting an assault when the weather turned bad.
- Past Perfect: By the time reinforcements arrived, the survivors had already withstood a fierce assault.
