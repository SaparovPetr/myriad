# Insist - настаивать

I have to insist!

1. insist on
2. insist that
3. insist upon
4. insist repeatedly
5. always insist
6. still insist
7. firmly insist

- I have insisted on seeing the manager before.
- He insisted on paying for the meal yesterday.
- She was insisting that everyone follow the rules when I arrived.
- They had insisted upon a full refund before the store opened.
