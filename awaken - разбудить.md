5. awaken consciousness

- Present Perfect: The old photograph has awakened long-forgotten memories of her childhood.
- Past Simple: The documentary awakened a deep interest in marine biology.
- Past Continuous: The smell of coffee was awakening my senses as I walked into the kitchen.
- Past Perfect: By the time the alarm sounded, the noise from the street had already awakened everyone in the house.
