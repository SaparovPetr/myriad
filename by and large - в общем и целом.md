# by and large - в общем и целом

1. Accept by and large
2. Agree by and large
3. True by and large
4. Successful by and large
5. Work by and large
6. By and large, it is
7. By and large, they are

- Present Perfect: The new policy has been accepted by and large, though there are some complaints.
- Past Simple: I agreed by and large with his assessment of the situation.
- Past Continuous: While the project was working by and large, a few critical bugs appeared.
- Past Perfect: They had found the report to be true by and large before the new evidence emerged.
