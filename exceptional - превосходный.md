# Exceptional - превосходный

1. Exceptional talent
2. Exceptional quality
3. Exceptional performance
4. Exceptional ability
5. Exceptional service
6. Exceptional work
7. Exceptional results

- Present Perfect: She has demonstrated exceptional talent in mathematics since her early school years.
- Past Simple: The chef prepared a meal of exceptional quality for the special event.
- Past Continuous: The team was delivering an exceptional performance until the main player got injured.
- Past Perfect: They had already received exceptional service before the manager even knew who the client was.
