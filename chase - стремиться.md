# Chase - стремиться

1. chase a dream
2. chase success
3. chase a goal
4. chase happiness
5. chase an opportunity
6. chase money
7. chase fame

- Present Perfect: She has chased her dream of becoming a pilot since she was a child.
- Past Simple: He chased the opportunity to work abroad and finally moved last year.
- Past Continuous: They were chasing success so relentlessly that they forgot to enjoy life.
- Past Perfect: By the time he retired, he had chased money for decades but found little satisfaction.
