# consideration - рассмотрение

1. Take into consideration
2. Give consideration to
3. Under consideration
4. Careful consideration
5. Due consideration
6. Full consideration
7. After due consideration


- The committee has already taken his proposal into consideration.
- The judge gave careful consideration to all the evidence.
- While they were discussing the plan, our application was under consideration.
- After they had given the matter full consideration, they made their decision.
