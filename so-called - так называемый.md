# so-called - так называемый

1. the so-called expert
2. a so-called friend
3. so-called freedom
4. so-called solution
5. so-called leader
6. so-called progress
7. the so-called problem

- He has been called a so-called expert on the topic, but he has never published any research.
- She dismissed the so-called friend after he betrayed her trust.
- They were protesting against the so-called freedom that only existed on paper.
- The government had already rejected the so-called solution before the meeting ended.
