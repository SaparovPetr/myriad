# budge - сдвинуться с места

1. refuse to budge
2. will not budge
3. wouldn't budge an inch
4. not budge an inch
5. never budge
6. barely budge
7. finally budge

- Despite all negotiations, the management has refused to budge on the salary issue.
- The heavy cabinet would not budge when we tried to move it.
- While we were pushing the car, its wheels were spinning but it wasn't budging.
- By the time help arrived, the stuck door had not budged at all.
