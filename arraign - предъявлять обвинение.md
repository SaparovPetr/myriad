# arraign - предъявлять обвинение

1. arraign someone for a crime
2. arraign the defendant
3. arraign on charges
4. arraign in court
5. arraign for murder
6. arraign the suspect
7. be arraigned on counts of

- Present Perfect: The prosecutor has arraigned the suspect on charges of fraud.
- Past Simple: The judge arraigned the defendant for theft last Thursday.
- Past Continuous: While the police were gathering evidence, the district attorney was arraigning another suspect in a separate case.
- Past Perfect: By the time the new evidence was found, the court had already arraigned him on the original charges.
