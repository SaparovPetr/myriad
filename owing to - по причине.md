# Owing to - по причине

- owing to the bad weather

1. owing to circumstances
2. owing to the fact
3. owing to bad weather
4. owing to a lack
5. owing to technical problems
6. owing to unforeseen events
7. owing to his efforts

- Present Perfect: The flight has been canceled owing to technical problems.
- Past Simple: The match was postponed yesterday owing to bad weather.
- Past Continuous: They were falling behind schedule owing to unforeseen events.
- Past Perfect: The project had succeeded owing to his efforts.
