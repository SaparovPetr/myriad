# Accordance - соответствие

1. In accordance with the rules
2. In accordance with the law
3. In accordance with the agreement
4. In accordance with the instructions
5. In accordance with the policy
6. In accordance with the regulations
7. In accordance with the requirements

- Present Perfect: The new procedures have been developed in accordance with the latest safety standards.
- Past Simple: He acted in accordance with the manager's direct orders.
- Past Continuous: The team was working in accordance with the old guidelines until last month.
- Past Perfect: The document had been prepared in accordance with the template before it was reviewed.
