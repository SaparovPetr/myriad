# roll in - нахлынуть; поступать в большом количестве

1. roll in on schedule
2. roll in from various places
3. roll in over the hills
4. roll in with the tide
5. roll in thick and fast
6. roll in unexpectedly
7. roll in one after another

- The new orders have rolled in steadily all week.
- The reports rolled in yesterday afternoon.
- The fog was rolling in from the sea as we left the beach.
- By the time the meeting started, complaints had already rolled in from several customers.
