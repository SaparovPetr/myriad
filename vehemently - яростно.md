# vehemently - яростно

1. vehemently oppose
2. vehemently deny
3. vehemently disagree
4. vehemently protest
5. vehemently argue
6. vehemently reject
7. vehemently defend

- Present Perfect: She has vehemently opposed the new policy since it was introduced.
- Past Simple: He vehemently denied the accusations during the trial yesterday.
- Past Continuous: They were vehemently arguing about the budget when the manager entered the room.
- Past Perfect: By the time the evidence was presented, the lawyer had vehemently rejected all previous claims.
