# outrun - опережать

Seven simple and popular collocations with "outrun" in the meaning "опережать":

1. outrun the competition  
2. outrun a rival  
3. outrun expectations  
4. outrun the police  
5. outrun danger  
6. outrun a deadline  
7. outrun abilities  

Examples in different tenses:

Present Perfect: She has outrun all her competitors this season.  
Past Simple: He outran the other runners in the race yesterday.  
Past Continuous: They were outrunning their opponents during the game.  
Past Perfect: By the time the race ended, she had already outrun everyone else.
