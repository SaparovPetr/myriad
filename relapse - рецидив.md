# relapse - рецидив

Seven simple and popular collocations with "relapse" in the meaning "рецидив":

1. suffer a relapse  
2. prevent relapse  
3. risk of relapse  
4. relapse into addiction  
5. experience a relapse  
6. relapse after recovery  
7. avoid relapse  

Examples in different tenses:

Present Perfect: He has suffered a relapse after months of sobriety.  
Past Simple: She relapsed shortly after completing the treatment.  
Past Continuous: They were trying to prevent him from relapsing.  
Past Perfect: By the time the doctor visited, he had already relapsed once.
