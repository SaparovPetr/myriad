# failure - неудачник


Seven simple and popular collocations with "failure" in the meaning "неудачник":

1. complete failure  
2. total failure  
3. career failure  
4. failure at something  
5. perceived failure  
6. personal failure  
7. failure in life  

Examples in different tenses:

Present Perfect: He has been seen as a failure by many.  
Past Simple: She was called a failure after the project collapsed.  
Past Continuous: They were feeling like failures during that difficult time.  
Past Perfect: He had considered himself a failure before finding success.
