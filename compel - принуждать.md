# compel - принуждать

1. Compel someone to do something
2. Feel compelled to do something
3. Compel obedience
4. Compel attention
5. Compel respect
6. Compel by law
7. Compel by force

- Present Perfect: The evidence has compelled the jury to reconsider their verdict.
- Past Simple: The urgent situation compelled her to act immediately.
- Past Continuous: The speaker was compelling the audience's attention with her powerful story.
- Past Perfect: The court had compelled him to testify before he decided to speak voluntarily.
