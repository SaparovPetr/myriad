# suspicion - подозрение; предположение

#### Common Collocations with "suspicion"  
1. strong suspicion  
2. reasonable suspicion  
3. growing suspicion  
4. deep suspicion  
5. baseless suspicion  
6. arouse suspicion  
7. confirm suspicion  

#### Verb Tenses Examples  
- Present Perfect: They have had a growing suspicion about his involvement.  
- Past Simple: She expressed suspicion regarding the unusual behavior.  
- Past Continuous: The detectives were operating under suspicion throughout the investigation.  
- Past Perfect: By the time the truth was revealed, everyone had already developed suspicion.
