# endanger - представлять угрозу

1. endanger lives
2. endanger health
3. endanger safety
4. endanger species
5. endanger the environment
6. endanger national security
7. endanger a mission

- Present Perfect: The factory's pollution has endangered the health of the entire community.
- Past Simple: His reckless driving endangered the lives of his passengers.
- Past Continuous: The explorers knew they were endangering the mission by splitting up.
- Past Perfect: By the time help arrived, the fire had already endangered several nearby homes.
