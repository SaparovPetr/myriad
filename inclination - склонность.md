# inclination - склонность

1. Natural inclination
2. Strong inclination
3. Political inclination
4. Artistic inclination
5. Inclination to believe
6. Inclination towards something
7. Show an inclination

- She has always had a natural inclination for music.
- He showed a strong inclination to study engineering from a young age.
- They have never shared his political inclination.
- The child was showing an artistic inclination while she was painting.
- By the time he finished the book, he had already felt an inclination to believe the author's theory.
