# bill - купюра

Seven simple and popular collocations with **"bill"** in the meaning **"купюра"**:

1. dollar bill  
2. five-dollar bill  
3. hundred-dollar bill  
4. paper bill  
5. large bill  
6. small bill  
7. fold a bill  

Examples in different tenses:

- Present Perfect: I have paid with a twenty-dollar bill many times.  
- Past Simple: She gave me a five-dollar bill for change.  
- Past Continuous: He was counting the bills carefully.  
- Past Perfect: By the time I arrived, they had already exchanged the bills.  

In Russian, **"bill"** as **"купюра"** refers to a paper banknote, such as a dollar bill or other currency notes.
