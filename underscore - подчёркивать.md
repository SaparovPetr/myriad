# underscore - подчёркивать

1. underscore the importance
2. underscore the need
3. underscore the point
4. underscore the difference
5. underscore the fact
6. underscore the value
7. underscore the significance


- The recent study has underscored the importance of early childhood education.
- The speaker underscored the need for immediate action during the conference.
- The manager was underscoring the main point when the fire alarm sounded.
- His testimony had underscored the difference between the two accounts before the jury deliberated.
