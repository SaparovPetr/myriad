1. pitch a ball

3. pitch a stone
4. pitch a tent
5. pitch garbage
6. pitch a rope
7. pitch someone out

Examples in different tenses:

- Present Perfect: He has pitched the ball so fast that the batter could not react.

- Past Simple: She pitched the coin into the fountain and made a wish.

- Past Continuous: The children were pitching stones into the river when their mother called them.

- Past Perfect: By the time the rain started, the campers had already pitched the tent in the clearing.
