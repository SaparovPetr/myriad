# overreach - переоценивать

1. overreach one's authority
2. overreach one's capabilities
3. overreach one's grasp
4. overreach the mark
5. overreach the budget
6. overreach the limits
7. overreach oneself

- The manager has overreached his authority by signing that contract without approval.
- The ambitious project overreached its budget by several million dollars.
- The company was overreaching its capabilities when it tried to expand into ten new markets at once.
- By the time they realized the error, the team had already overreached the agreed limits of the investigation.
