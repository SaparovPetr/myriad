# yet - тем не менее

1. and yet
2. but yet
3. yet again
4. yet another
5. as yet
6. not yet
7. just yet

- Present Perfect: She has failed many times, and yet she continues to try.
- Past Simple: The weather was cold and rainy, but yet we had a wonderful trip.
- Past Continuous: He was explaining the rules yet again while everyone was listening.
- Past Perfect: They had searched the entire house, and yet they still hadn't found the keys.
