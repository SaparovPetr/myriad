# deliberate - осторожный; обдуманный

1. deliberate decision
2. deliberate action
3. deliberate attempt
4. deliberate pace
5. deliberate choice
6. deliberate effort
7. deliberate lie

- Present Perfect: The committee has made a deliberate decision to postpone the vote.
- Past Simple: He walked at a deliberate pace to the front of the hall.
- Past Continuous: She was making a deliberate effort to ignore the noise outside.
- Past Perfect: They had taken deliberate action before the situation worsened.
