# slip up - совершить ошибку

1. slip up and say
2. slip up badly
3. slip up on the details
4. a careless slip up
5. a major slip up
6. a minor slip up
7. make a slip up

- Present Perfect: He has slipped up and revealed the surprise party to the guest of honor.
- Past Simple: I slipped up badly on the final question of the exam.
- Past Continuous: While she was giving the speech, she was slipping up on the important dates.
- Past Perfect: The witness had slipped up on the details during the first interview, so the lawyer questioned him again.
