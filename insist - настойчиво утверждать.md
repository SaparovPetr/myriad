# insist - настойчиво утверждать

1. insist on
2. insist that
3. insist upon
4. insist firmly
5. insist repeatedly
6. still insist

- She has insisted on arriving early for every meeting this month. (Present Perfect)
- He insisted that the door was locked when he left. (Past Simple)
- They were insisting that they had seen the accident when the police arrived. (Past Continuous)
- By the time she showed the proof, he had insisted on his version of the story for an hour. (Past Perfect)
