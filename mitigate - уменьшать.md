# mitigate - уменьшать

1. mitigate the effects
2. mitigate the risk
3. mitigate the damage
4. mitigate the impact
5. mitigate the problem
6. mitigate the consequences
7. mitigate a threat

- The government has mitigated the effects of the flood by building stronger barriers.
- The new protocol mitigated the risk of a data breach last year.
- While the team was mitigating the damage, the fire continued to spread.
- By the time help arrived, the volunteers had already mitigated the worst of the impact.
