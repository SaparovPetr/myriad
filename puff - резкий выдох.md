# puff - резкий выдох

1. let out a puff
2. a puff of air
3. a puff of smoke
4. a puff of wind
5. a puff of dust
6. a puff of breath
7. take a puff

- Present Perfect: She has let out a weary puff after finishing the long report.
- Past Simple: He gave a puff of frustration when the computer crashed.
- Past Continuous: The old train was emitting steady puffs of black smoke as it climbed the hill.
- Past Perfect: By the time we arrived, the dragon had vanished, leaving only a final puff of smoke in the air.
