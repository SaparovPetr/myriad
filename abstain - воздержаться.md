# abstain - воздержаться

1. abstain from voting
2. abstain from alcohol
3. abstain from commenting
4. completely abstain
5. choose to abstain
6. abstain altogether
7. abstain from eating

- Present Perfect: The committee member has abstained from voting on the controversial issue.
- Past Simple: He abstained from alcohol for the entire month of January.
- Past Continuous: During the debate, she was abstaining from commenting until she had heard all the arguments.
- Past Perfect: The delegate had already abstained from the final vote before the results were announced.
