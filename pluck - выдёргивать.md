# Pluck - выдергивать

1. pluck a hair
2. pluck a flower
3. pluck a chicken
4. pluck eyebrows
5. pluck a string
6. pluck from obscurity
7. pluck up courage

- She has just plucked a few flowers from the garden for the table.
- He plucked the guitar string gently to check the tune.
- The barber was carefully plucking the stray hairs from the customer's neck.
- By the time the rescue team arrived, they had already plucked all the survivors from the wreckage.
