

Here are 4 common collocations with "to do whatever it takes":

- I'll do whatever it takes to succeed.
- He's willing to do whatever it takes to win.
- She did whatever it took to provide for her family.
- They are ready to do whatever it takes to get the job done.

And here is an example sentence using "to do whatever it takes" in different tenses:

Present Perfect:

The team has done whatever it takes to reach the finals this year.

Past Simple:

The actor did whatever it took to prepare for his role in the film.

Past Continuous:

The firefighters were doing whatever it took to contain the forest fire.

Past Perfect:

The scientist had done whatever it took to find a cure for the disease.
