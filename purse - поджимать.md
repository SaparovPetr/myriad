# purse - поджимать

Seven simple and popular collocations with "purse" in the meaning "поджимать" (to tighten or contract, usually lips):

1. purse lips
2. purse mouth
3. purse your lips
4. purse tightly
5. purse slightly
6. purse nervously
7. purse in disapproval

Examples in different tenses:

Present Perfect: She has pursed her lips every time she hears bad news.  
Past Simple: He pursed his lips when he saw the mess.  
Past Continuous: They were pursing their lips during the serious discussion.  
Past Perfect: By the time I arrived, she had already pursed her lips in frustration.
