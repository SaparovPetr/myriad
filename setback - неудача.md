# setback - неудача

1. suffer a setback
2. face a setback
3. experience a setback
4. overcome a setback
5. be a setback
6. deal with a setback
7. cause a setback

- Present Perfect: The team has suffered a major setback with the loss of their star player.
- Past Simple: Our company faced a serious setback when the funding was cancelled.
- Past Continuous: They were experiencing a setback while the software kept crashing during the final test.
- Past Perfect: By the time the new manager arrived, the project had already overcome a significant setback.
