Collocations:

- conquer an enemy
- conquer a people
- conquer fear
- conquer the world

Example sentences: 

Present Perfect:

Past Simple:

Past Continuous:
The rising empire was conquering territory after territory in its quest to expand its borders.

Past Perfect:
For years, the general had conquered his enemies with superior tactics and discipline.
