# graciously - любезно

#### Common Collocations with "graciously"  
1. graciously accepted  
2. graciously offered  
3. graciously responded  
4. graciously thanked  
5. graciously invited  
6. graciously forgiven  
7. graciously welcomed  

#### Verb Tenses Examples  
- Present Perfect: She has graciously accepted all the compliments.  
- Past Simple: He graciously offered his help during the event.  
- Past Continuous: They were graciously responding to the guests’ questions.  
- Past Perfect: By the time the meeting ended, she had graciously thanked everyone.
