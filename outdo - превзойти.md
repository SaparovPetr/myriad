# outdo - превзойти

1. Outdo the competition
2. Outdo oneself
3. Outdo expectations
4. Outdo a rival
5. Outdo a previous record
6. Outdo everyone else
7. Outdo all others

- Present Perfect: She has outdone all her previous sales records this quarter.
- Past Simple: He easily outdid his main rival in the final race.
- Past Continuous: They were constantly trying to outdo each other in every project.
- Past Perfect: By the time the awards were announced, the new film had already outdone all expectations at the box office.
