# expense - расходы

Seven simple and popular collocations with **"expense"** in the meaning **"расходы"**:

1. business expenses  
2. travel expenses  
3. medical expenses  
4. operating expenses  
5. unexpected expenses  
6. monthly expenses  
7. reduce expenses  

Examples in different tenses:

- Present Perfect: They have reduced their expenses significantly this year.  
- Past Simple: She paid all the travel expenses last month.  
- Past Continuous: The company was tracking its expenses carefully during the quarter.  
- Past Perfect: By the end of the year, they had already covered all the expenses.  

In Russian, **"expense"** is commonly translated as **"расходы"**, referring to costs or expenditures, especially those related to business or personal spending.
