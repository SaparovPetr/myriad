# shuffle - волочить ноги

#### Meaning Clarification  
The verb **"shuffle"** primarily means **to drag or slide the feet along the ground while walking**, which can be understood as "волочить ноги" in Russian.

#### Common Collocations with "shuffle"  
1. shuffle along  
2. shuffle feet  
3. shuffle slowly  
4. shuffle nervously  
5. shuffle around  
6. shuffle forward  
7. shuffle past  

#### Verb Tenses Examples  
- Present Perfect: He has shuffled his feet nervously during the meeting.  
- Past Simple: She shuffled along the hallway quietly.  
- Past Continuous: They were shuffling their feet while waiting in line.  
- Past Perfect: By the time the music started, he had already shuffled to the front.
