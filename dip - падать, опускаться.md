# dip - падать, опускаться

Seven simple and popular collocations with **"dip"** in the meaning **"падать, опускаться"**:

1. dip suddenly  
2. dip sharply  
3. dip slightly  
4. dip in temperature  
5. dip in sales  
6. dip below zero  
7. dip in performance  

Examples in different tenses:

- Present Perfect: Prices have dipped significantly this month.  
- Past Simple: The temperature dipped last night.  
- Past Continuous: Sales were dipping during the holiday season.  
- Past Perfect: By the time the report was released, the stocks had already dipped.
