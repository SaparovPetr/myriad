# Seldom - редко

She seldom gives interviews.

#### Common Collocations with "seldom"  
1. seldom seen  
2. seldom heard  
3. seldom visited  
4. seldom happens  
5. seldom used  
6. seldom noticed  
7. seldom remembered  

#### Verb Tenses Examples  
- Present Perfect: She has seldom visited this place before.  
- Past Simple: He seldom attended the meetings last year.  
- Past Continuous: They were seldom going out during the winter.  
- Past Perfect: By the time I moved, she had seldom called me.
