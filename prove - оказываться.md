# prove - оказываться

1. prove effective
2. prove useful
3. prove difficult
4. prove popular
5. prove impossible
6. prove successful
7. prove correct

- Present Perfect: The new software has proven effective in reducing customer complaints.
- Past Simple: The test proved the new method was more efficient.
- Past Continuous: While the team was testing the device, it was proving difficult to get accurate readings.
- Past Perfect: By the time the project ended, the original plan had proven impossible to follow.
