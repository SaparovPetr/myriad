# on the plus side - с позитивной стороны

1. on the plus side, at least
2. on the plus side, though
3. on the plus side, however
4. on the plus side, it could be worse
5. on the plus side, we have
6. on the plus side, the good news is
7. on the plus side, there is

- Present Perfect: On the plus side, I have already finished the main part of the project.
- Past Simple: On the plus side, the weather was warm for the whole trip.
- Past Continuous: On the plus side, they were offering free samples at the market that day.
- Past Perfect: On the plus side, she had already booked the tickets before the prices went up.
