# duly - должным образом

1. duly noted
2. duly appointed
3. duly authorized
4. duly elected
5. duly signed
6. duly processed
7. duly considered

- Present Perfect: The committee has duly noted all the concerns raised during the public hearing.
- Past Simple: The secretary duly signed the contract after both parties agreed.
- Past Continuous: The manager was duly processing the paperwork when the system crashed.
- Past Perfect: The officer had already duly authorized the payment before the audit began.
