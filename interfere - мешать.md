# Interfere - мешать

Don't interfere me!

1. Interfere with
2. Interfere in
3. Interfere directly
4. Constantly interfere
5. Unwilling to interfere
6. Refuse to interfere
7. Deliberately interfere

- Present Perfect: The bad weather has interfered with our travel plans.
- Past Simple: The teacher interfered in the argument between the students.
- Past Continuous: He was interfering constantly, which made the task impossible.
- Past Perfect: By the time the manager arrived, the faulty software had already interfered with the system.
