# overlook - пропускать

1. overlook a mistake
2. overlook a detail
3. overlook the obvious
4. overlook a problem
5. overlook a fact
6. overlook an error
7. overlook a fault

- Present Perfect: The manager has overlooked the same error in the last three reports.
- Past Simple: She overlooked a crucial detail in the contract.
- Past Continuous: He was overlooking the obvious problem while focusing on minor issues.
- Past Perfect: They had overlooked the warning signs before the system failed.
