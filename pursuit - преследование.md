# pursuit - преследование

Seven simple and popular collocations with "pursuit" in the meaning "преследование":

1. in pursuit of  
2. high-speed pursuit  
3. police pursuit  
4. relentless pursuit  
5. pursuit of justice  
6. pursuit of a criminal  
7. pursuit by authorities  

Examples in different tenses:

Present Perfect: The police have been in pursuit of the suspect for hours.  
Past Simple: They pursued the thief through the crowded streets.  
Past Continuous: The officers were pursuing the suspect when he disappeared.  
Past Perfect: By the time backup arrived, they had already pursued the criminal for miles.
