# boast - хвастовство

1. empty boast
2. idle boast
3. proud boast
4. vain boast
5. wild boast
6. mere boast

- I have made an empty boast about my singing ability.
- He made an idle boast that he could run faster than anyone in the school.
- She was making a proud boast about her son's grades when the principal called him to the office.
- They had made a wild boast about finishing the project in a week, but it actually took them a month.
