# Omen - знамение

- Troubling omen

1. good omen  
2. bad omen  
3. ominous omen  
4. sign or omen  
5. take as an omen  
6. foreboding omen  
7. prophetic omen  

Examples in different tenses:

- Present Perfect: They have taken the strange event as a bad omen.  
- Past Simple: The black cat was considered a bad omen.  
- Past Continuous: People were watching the sky, looking for any omen.  
- Past Perfect: By the time the ceremony started, many had already seen the omen.  

The word "omen" originally comes from Latin and refers to a sign or indication, often believed to predict the future, either favorably or unfavorably.
