# exaggeration - преувеличение

1. gross exaggeration
2. wild exaggeration
3. slight exaggeration
4. obvious exaggeration
5. typical exaggeration
6. pure exaggeration
7. deliberate exaggeration

- Present Perfect: She has often been accused of gross exaggeration in her stories.
- Past Simple: He made a wild exaggeration about the size of the fish he caught.
- Past Continuous: I knew you were engaging in a slight exaggeration when you described the queue.
- Past Perfect: By the time he clarified, everyone had already noticed the obvious exaggeration in his claim.
