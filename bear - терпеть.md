# Bear - терпеть

- bear a toothache

1. Bear the pain
2. Bear the cost
3. Bear the burden
4. Bear a grudge
5. Bear responsibility
6. Bear the heat
7. Bear witness

- She has borne the pain of that injury for years.
- He bore the cost of the repairs himself.
- They were bearing the burden of the secret when the phone rang.
- She had borne a grudge against him long before they finally argued.
