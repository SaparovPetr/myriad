# adopter - приверженец

1. early adopter
2. new adopter
3. first-time adopter
4. enthusiastic adopter
5. quick adopter
6. recent adopter
7. willing adopter

- Present Perfect: She has been an early adopter of every new smartphone model for years.
- Past Simple: He became a willing adopter of the minimalist lifestyle after reading the book.
- Past Continuous: They were being enthusiastic adopters of the trend before it became mainstream.
- Past Perfect: By the time the software was public, she had already been a recent adopter of its beta version.
