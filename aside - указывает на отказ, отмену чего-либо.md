# aside - указывает на отказ, отмену чего-либо

1. Set aside
2. Put aside
3. Lay aside
4. Brush aside
5. Push aside
6. Cast aside
7. Leave aside

- She has set aside her plans for the weekend.
- He put aside his book when the phone rang.
- They were brushing aside all objections during the meeting.
- By the time he apologized, she had already cast aside his earlier comments.
