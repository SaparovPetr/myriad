# Boldly - смело

- going boldly
- Move boldly

We must struggle with those tasks boldly.

1. Boldly go
2. Boldly state
3. Boldly claim
4. Boldly declare
5. Boldly assert
6. Boldly step
7. Boldly announce

- She has boldly gone where no one from her team has explored before.
- He boldly stated his opinion during the meeting yesterday.
- While others hesitated, they were boldly stepping forward into the unknown.
- By the time the truth emerged, he had already boldly declared his innocence to the public.
