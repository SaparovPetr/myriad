# ruthlessness - беспощадность

1. sheer ruthlessness
2. cold ruthlessness
3. calculated ruthlessness
4. complete ruthlessness
5. with ruthlessness
6. act of ruthlessness
7. show ruthlessness

- Present Perfect: The manager has shown complete ruthlessness by firing the entire team without warning.
- Past Simple: The dictator acted with cold ruthlessness when he ordered the arrests.
- Past Continuous: The general was demonstrating sheer ruthlessness as he ignored all pleas for mercy.
- Past Perfect: By the time the truce was signed, the warlord had already displayed calculated ruthlessness in every battle.
