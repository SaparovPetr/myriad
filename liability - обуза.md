# liability - обуза

1. be a liability
2. become a liability
3. turn into a liability
4. prove a liability
5. consider someone a liability
6. view someone as a liability
7. treat someone like a liability

- Considering his poor health, he has been a liability to the expedition team for the entire journey.
- Last year, the old machinery proved a liability when it caused a major delay in production.
- During the project, the inexperienced intern was constantly making mistakes and becoming a liability to the whole department.
- After his failed negotiation, the manager realized that the reckless advisor had been a liability for the company all along.
