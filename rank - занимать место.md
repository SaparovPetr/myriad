# Rank - занимать место


1. to rank first
2. to rank high
3. to rank among the best
4. to rank number one
5. to rank in the top ten
6. to rank low
7. to rank last

- She has ranked first in her class for three consecutive semesters.
- He ranked second in the national competition last year.
- While they were ranking the applications, the power went out.
- By the time the final results were posted, I had already ranked lower than I expected.
