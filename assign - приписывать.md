2. assign value
3. assign responsibility
4. assign meaning
5. assign a role
6. assign a task
7. assign a grade

- Present Perfect: The committee has assigned blame for the mistake to the new manager.
- Past Simple: The teacher assigned a specific role to each student in the play yesterday.
- Past Continuous: At that moment, the director was assigning the most difficult task to the senior team.
- Past Perfect: By the time the meeting ended, the board had already assigned responsibility for the project to her.
