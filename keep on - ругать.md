# Keep on - ругать

She kept on him about smoking.

Collocations:
1. keep on at
2. keep on about
3. keep on and on
4. keep on someone
5. keep on doing something
6. keep on at someone
7. keep on nagging

Examples:
- Present Perfect: She has kept on at her son about his grades all week.
- Past Simple: He kept on about the broken window for ten minutes.
- Past Continuous: At dinner last night, they were keeping on their brother for being late.
- Past Perfect: By the time I arrived, she had already kept on and on about the dirty dishes.
