# afterwards - после

1. shortly afterwards
2. immediately afterwards
3. soon afterwards
4. not long afterwards
5. days afterwards
6. years afterwards
7. just afterwards

- Present Perfect: She has left the office, and I will call her shortly afterwards.
- Past Simple: He finished his meal and immediately afterwards went for a walk.
- Past Continuous: The power was restored, and soon afterwards people were celebrating in the streets.
- Past Perfect: The letter arrived not long afterwards, although I had already moved house.
